# User Interview Script

Use this for three real conversations. Keep each call to 10-15 minutes.

## Target

Founder, CTO, VP Engineering, engineering manager, or technical operator at a startup using at least one AI tool for coding, writing, research, data, or product features.

## Questions

1. Which AI tools are you paying for right now, and who owns the bill?
2. When did your AI spend last surprise you?
3. Do you know how many paid seats are active versus just assigned?
4. Have you compared plans or alternatives in the last 60 days?
5. What would make you trust or distrust an automated AI spend audit?
6. If the audit found meaningful savings, what would you do next?
7. Would you rather switch tools, downgrade plans, or keep tools and buy discounted credits?

## Notes Format

```md

**Role / company stage:** ...
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Context:** ...

**Direct quotes:**
- "..."
- "..."
- "..."

**Most surprising thing:** ...
**What changed about the design:** ...
```

## Good Signals

- A specific invoice amount.
- A contradictory behavior, such as caring about savings but refusing tool churn.
- A real procurement constraint.
- A quote with unusual wording you would not invent.
